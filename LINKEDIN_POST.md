# LinkedIn Post Draft

I built an **Academic Timetable and Institutional Workload Dashboard** in Microsoft Power BI to connect two common academic needs: finding the correct timetable and understanding the workload behind it.

### The problem

Timetable information is often distributed across static sheets, links and departmental records. That makes both schedule lookup and management-level comparison slower than necessary.

### The reporting story

The dashboard follows a **lookup-to-management** journey:

- **MAIN** provides a clear entry point.
- **TIMETABLE** narrows the schedule using school, department, programme, year and semester/division filters.
- **ANALYSIS** explains department distribution, theory versus practical hours, laboratories, faculty strength and class-in-charge responsibility.
